Due Now guides you in planning your week right.

Set priority and difficulty.

Avoid the hassle of having to constantly schedule your tasks in a certain period of time. Just specify your tasks’ level of difficulty and priority as you input your tasks into the app, and let the app do the categorizing ans scheduling for you.

Get notified of deadlines.

Get desktop notifications whenever deadlines are near, or when there are postponed tasks that are should be finished already.

Know when to start your tasks.

Be able to plan out your strategy in conquering that giant pile of work by knowing the most efficient time to start your tasks. Aside from the wide array of possible times to start derived from your tasks» details, you can also set the time and make it work for you.
